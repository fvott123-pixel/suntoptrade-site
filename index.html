Discuss a Private Sourcing Project

Honest delivery timelines — order early.

China-to-AU sourcing takes longer than most buyers expect. Allow realistic lead times.

Sea freight, stock or quick-turn orders: 8–14 weeks door-to-door from order confirmation.
Custom-manufactured orders (bespoke kitchens, custom furniture, complex AV systems): 14–20 weeks or longer — factory production alone can run 8–12 weeks before shipment.
Air freight (urgent orders): 4–8 weeks door-to-door. Cost: 3–5× sea freight. Only viable for high-value, low-volume goods.

Sun Top does not guarantee specific delivery dates. We deliver as quickly as the supply chain allows and keep you updated through production.

GST treatment — your choice.

Sun Top Trade Co., Limited is registered in Hong Kong, where no GST or VAT applies. Sun Top invoices do not include GST.

Import GST of approximately 10% on landed value is payable to Australian customs at port. The buyer is named as importer of record on every shipment.

  • GST-registered, claiming the GST back: Reclaim import GST via your BAS as a normal input tax credit. Net cost = landed price.
  • GST-registered, not claiming (simpler): Many buyers prefer to skip the BAS paperwork and treat the import GST as a cost. Faster, cleaner, no extra admin — your landed cost stays exactly as quoted.
  • Private buyers and non-GST-registered: Import GST is part of your landed cost and cannot be claimed back.

Sun Top's licensed Australian customs broker handles all import paperwork on your behalf. The amount of import GST owed is shown in every quote — claim it or don't, your choice.
