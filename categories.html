Active lane detail

What "active lane" actually means.

For these categories Sun Top already has verified factory relationships, current pricing, and recent quote turnaround data.

Commercial Furniture & Retail Fixtures

Tables, seating, gondola units, slatwall, checkout counters, display cabinets, hospitality and office fit-out furniture. Sun Top has vetted factory relationships across Guangdong (Foshan, Guangzhou) and Suzhou supplying the Australian hospitality and retail sectors.

Typical lead time door-to-door: 10–14 weeks. Quote turnaround: 5–10 business days.

Shop & Supermarket Fitouts

Turnkey retail and supermarket fitouts: gondola shelving, checkout counters, joinery, custom cabinetry, LED lighting packages, signage, POS hardware, and refrigeration cases on request. Sourced from established China supermarket-fit-out clusters supplying AU independents, chains, and franchise rollouts.

Typical lead time door-to-door: 12–16 weeks. Quote turnaround: 7–14 business days. Sun Top can structure multi-site rollouts as a single rolling sourcing programme to lock in factory pricing and freight slots.

Designer Kitchens & Joinery (sintered stone benchtops, cabinetry)

Italian-style kitchen cabinetry and large-format sintered stone benchtops sourced from Yunfu, China's largest stone-processing cluster. Suited to designer kitchen retailers, custom-home builders, and high-end residential projects.

Typical lead time door-to-door: 12–14 weeks. Quote turnaround: 7–14 business days.

LED Commercial Lighting

LED track lights, downlights, panel lights, linear lights, decorative commercial fixtures. Sourced from Guzhen, China's LED lighting capital, where Sun Top maintains office presence for factory visits and price negotiation.

Typical lead time door-to-door: 10–12 weeks. Quote turnaround: 5–10 business days. SAA approval verification is mandatory before any AU sample order.

Cleaning & Facilities Equipment

Ride-on sweepers, walk-behind scrubbers, industrial cleaning trolleys, facilities maintenance equipment. Suited to retail groups, warehouse operators, facilities management companies.

Typical lead time door-to-door: 10–14 weeks. Quote turnaround: 5–10 business days.

Luxury Furniture (private clients)

Premium sofas, beds, dining sets, designer furniture for private residential buyers. Sun Top sources directly from the same factories that supply European and Australian designer brands at retail.

Minimum order: $5,000 AUD. Typical lead time door-to-door: 12–14 weeks. Quote turnaround: 7–14 business days.

Custom Sourcing — Hi-Fi, Watches, Art, Vehicles

For categories outside our active lanes — premium hi-fi systems, watches, art, vehicles, niche luxury goods — Sun Top begins a fresh factory shortlist and full verification cycle.

Quote turnaround: 10–20 business days. Lead time varies by category. We will tell you honestly whether a category is viable from China within your budget and timeline before charging the sourcing engagement fee.
