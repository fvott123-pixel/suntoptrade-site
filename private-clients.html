For Private Clients

Sourcing high-ticket items for private buyers.

Sun Top serves private clients sourcing premium goods direct from China. Luxury furniture, hi-fi systems, designer kitchens, watches, art, vehicles, and other high-value items. Same trading model as our business clients — factory-direct pricing, door-to-door AUD delivery, full Sun Top accountability.

Minimum order: $5,000 AUD per project.

The minimum can be a single luxury item or multiple items bundled into one project reaching the threshold. Below $5,000 the freight and customs overhead means there is no meaningful saving to pass on.

Same operating model. Same disclosures.

Private clients use the same Sun Top operating model as our business clients:

  • Sourcing engagement fee at project start, credited against your final invoice
  • Door-to-door delivery in AUD to your nominated address
  • You unload, unpack, dispose of packaging
  • You are named as importer of record
  • Import GST is part of your landed cost (private buyers cannot reclaim GST)
  • Sea freight takes 8–14 weeks door-to-door; custom-build items can run 14–20 weeks or longer
  • Australian Consumer Law non-excludable guarantees apply
